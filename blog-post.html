Namaste.

From my mat, to yours, welcome. Since you're here to learn about who I am, let me tell you how I began my yoga journey.. At the age of 22 I was in a car accident that could have been fatal for me if I didn't have such good fortune or any spirits watching over me.

To this day I am sincerely thankful that I survived nearly unscathed, and shortly after that I took it upon myself to show gratitude to the Higher Powers of this Universe for letting me continue to be worthy of their love.

I started going to church for the first time in my life, and was uplifted by the joy and compassionI experienced from the Reverend in my hometown. I may have also been crushing a little too though, as my Reverend was a young, beautiful aboriginal woman and I never thought church would ever be like that. But since then, I have not experienced that same joy and compassion in a service.

Anyway, with my uplifted spirit, inspired to learn more about this Being I was hearing about who goes by the name of God, I went to my local library and started to look up some books on Him.

My first search attempts were unsuccessful as I tried to find books with the search term 'God', but when I replaced that with 'Soul' I came across a book named "God in All Worlds: An Anthology of Contemporary Spiritual Writing" by Lucinda Vardey.

I spent months poring over it.

Some days I would read a few excerpts, other days a few chapters.

Sometimes I had a hard time digesting the content, and it definitely led me to having a total spiritual crisis. Probably close to a third or halfway through the book was when I found a hidden gem of a thinker and decided YES, I resonate with you.

His name was Paramhansa Yogananda.

I still rely heavily on the philosophy of his that was shared through this book. After consistently being reminded throughout the book of how deeply I felt connected to his words, I set myself on the journey to try yoga. My first class really was an awakening for me, as I felt myself in my body for what felt like the first time in my life.
